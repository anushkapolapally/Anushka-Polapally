Anushka Polapally is a high school senior from Katy, TX. She is passionate about solving people-centered problems through the thoughtful design of systems, both digital and organizational.

After seeing the underutilization of diverse volunteering opportunities in her community, she founded Peared, an organization that empowers both student-organizations and volunteers in order to encourage meaningful civic engagement.

Recognized by several competitions including Blue Ocean Entrepreneur Competition, Conrad Challenge, and Diamond Challenge, she has demonstrated excellence in creating innovations with the potential for significant social impact.

Anushka has a strong background in computational problem solving, through her extensive experience in competitive mathematics and computer science. She utilizes the problem-solving skills from these fields to inform her approach to tackling complex, real-world challenges.

Currently, she is working on developing optimal iterative refinement pipelines for open-source LLMs. This project was started at the NYU Agentic AI Lab with the assistance of the NYU GSTEM program. Through this work, she hopes to democratize high-quality LLM usage and allow for user-data transparency.
