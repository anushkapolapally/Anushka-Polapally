Peared

After noticing many underutilized volunteering opportunities from student organizations, I created Peared.

Peared is a platform designed to empower both student organizations and volunteers, encouraging thoughtful and personalized community service.

It provides a centralized hub for volunteer sign-ups, consolidating a wide range of opportunities in one place.

Organizations can use Peared to showcase upcoming events, request volunteers, and share their missions through our forum.

To empower small student-run organizations to create a larger impact, we provide: promotion of the org on our social media, creation of promotional materials, microgrants to cover material costs, and event logistic assistance.

Peared's work in encouraging civic engagement has been featured on NPR, Civics Unplugged, and Bold Journey Magazine.
