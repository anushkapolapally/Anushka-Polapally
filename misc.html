Numberlink

Numberlink puzzles involve connecting several nodes by a grid in a way that they do not intersect. I have been playing Numberlink for around 10 years, starting through the app Flow Free. After exhausting all of those puzzles, I now play on the Puzzle Barron website and I am in the Puzzle Barron's Hall of Fame for Numberlink. I also hold the 5th fastest 5x5 30 second Flow Free time, but prefer to play as large of a grid size as possible.
